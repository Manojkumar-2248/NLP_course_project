RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on 1st January 2026 between Mr. Rahul Sharma ("Landlord") and Ms. Priya Verma ("Tenant").

1. PROPERTY
The Landlord hereby agrees to rent the residential property located at Flat No. 402, Green Heights Apartments, Hyderabad, Telangana to the Tenant for residential purposes only.

2. TERM
The lease term shall commence on 1st January 2026 and shall continue for a period of eleven (11) months. This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive terms of eleven (11) months unless either party provides written notice at least thirty (30) days prior to the expiration date.

3. RENT
The Tenant agrees to pay a monthly rent of INR 18,000 (Eighteen Thousand Rupees only), payable on or before the 5th day of each month via bank transfer to the Landlord's designated account.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a refundable security deposit of INR 50,000. The deposit shall be returned within sixty (60) days after termination of the lease, subject to deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

5. LATE PAYMENT PENALTY
If the Tenant fails to pay rent within five (5) days after the due date, a late fee of INR 500 per day shall be charged. Continued non-payment beyond fifteen (15) days may result in termination of this Agreement.

6. TERMINATION
The Landlord may terminate this Agreement without prior notice in the event of unlawful activities, property damage, or breach of contract terms. The Tenant may terminate this Agreement by providing sixty (60) days written notice.

7. MAINTENANCE AND DAMAGES
The Tenant shall be responsible for maintaining the property in good condition. Any structural damages caused due to negligence shall be compensated by the Tenant.

8. LIABILITY
The Landlord shall not be liable for any personal injury, loss, or damage to personal belongings occurring within the premises.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
